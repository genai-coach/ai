## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Are the sessions really free?**  
A: Yes! Regular bi-weekly Agentic Live sessions are completely free. Some special workshops may have a small fee to cover costs.

**Q: Do I need to be an expert to join?**  
A: Not at all! We welcome everyone from beginners to experts. Sessions are designed to provide value at multiple levels.

**Q: What if I can't attend live?**  
A: All sessions are recorded and made available to community members within 24 hours.

**Q: How do I book a 1:1 session?**  
A: Simply email us with your availability and specific topics you'd like to cover. We'll coordinate scheduling and send confirmation.

**Q: Can sessions be conducted in multiple languages?**  
A: Each session is typically conducted in one language, but we rotate between Danish, English, and Italian based on community needs.

**Q: Is this only for developers?**  
A: While technical knowledge helps, we welcome anyone interested in agentic AI - product managers, business analysts, and curious learners are all welcome!
